# Select Obsidian As Knowledge Platform

Date: 2026-06-14

## Decision

Obsidian will be the primary interface for viewing and editing Wallie's knowledge base.

## Reason

- Markdown native
- Local-first
- Long-term sustainability
- Excellent linking capabilities

## Consequences

### Positive

- Human-readable knowledge
- Easy backups
- Independence from proprietary platforms

### Negative

- Requires discipline in note organization

## Status

Approved
